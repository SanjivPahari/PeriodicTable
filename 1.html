Basic Information about the element

Name: Hydrogen
Symbol: H
Atomic Number: 1

1s1

The chemical element hydrogen is classed as a nonmetal.
It can become metallic at very high pressures.
It was discovered in 1766 by Henry Cavendish.

Number of Protons/Electrons: 1
Number of Neutrons: 0
Atomic Mass: 1.00794 amu
Melting Point: -259.14 °C
Boiling Point: -252.87 °C
Classification: Non-metal
Uses: Filled In Baloons, Hydrogenation, Weilding
