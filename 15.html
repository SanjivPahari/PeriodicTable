Basic Information about the element

Name: Phosphorus
Symbol: P
Atomic Number: 15

1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p3

The chemical element phosphorus is classed as a nonmetal.
It was discovered in 1669 by Hennig Brand.

Number of Protons/Electrons: 15
Number of Neutrons: 16
Atomic Mass: 30.97376 amu
Melting Point: 44.1 °C
Boiling Point: 280 °C
Classification: Non-metal
Uses: For making fertilizers and detergents
