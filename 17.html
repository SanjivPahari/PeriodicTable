Basic Information about the element

Name: Chlorine
Symbol: Cl
Atomic Number: 17

1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p5

The chemical element chlorine is classed as halogen and a nonmetal.
It was discovered in 1774 by Carl Wilhelm Scheele.

Number of Protons/Electrons: 17
Number of Neutrons: 18
Atomic Mass: 35.4527 amu
Melting Point: -100.98 °C
Boiling Point: -34.6 °C
Classification: Halogen
Uses: Water purification, bleaches
