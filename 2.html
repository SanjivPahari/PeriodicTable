Basic Information about the element

Name: Helium
Symbol: He
Atomic Number: 2

The chemical element helium is classed as a noble gas and a nonmetal.
It was discovered in 1895 by William Ramsay.

Number of Protons/Electrons: 2
Number of Neutrons: 2
Atomic Mass: 4.002602 amu

Boiling Point: -268.6 °C
Classification: Noble Gas
