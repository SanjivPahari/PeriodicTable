Basic Information about the element

Name: Chromium
Symbol: Cr
Atomic Number: 24

The chemical element Chromium is classed as a transition metal.
It was discovered in 1797 by Louis Vauquelin.
Number of Protons/Electrons: 24
Number of Neutrons: 28
Atomic Mass: 51.9961 amu
Melting Point: 1857.0 °C (2130.15 K, 3374.6 °F)
Boiling Point: 2672.0 °C (2945.15 K, 4841.6 °F)
Classification: Transition metal
Uses: It is used for stainless steel
