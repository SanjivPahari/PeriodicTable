Basic Information about the element

Name: Zirconium
Symbol: Zr
Atomic Number: 40

1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4p6 4d2 5s2

The chemical element zirconium is classed as an transition metal.
It was discovered in 1789 by Martin Klaproth.
Number of Protons/Electrons: 40
Number of Neutrons: 51
Atomic Mass: 91.224 amu
Melting Point: 1852.0 °C
Boiling Point: 4377.0 °C
Classification: Transition Metal
Uses: Zirconium is used for nuclear applications.
