Basic Information about the element

Name: Aluminium
Symbol: Al
Atomic Number: 13

1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p1

The chemical element aluminum is classed as an other metal.
It was discovered in the 1750s by Andreas Marggraf.

Number of Protons/Electrons: 13
Number of Neutrons: 14
Atomic Mass: 26.981539 amu
Melting Point: 660.37 °C
Boiling Point: 2467 °C
Classification: Post-transition Metal
Uses: Making parts of aircraft, soda cans
