Basic Information about the element

Name: Argon
Symbol: Ar
Atomic Number: 18

1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6

The chemical element argon is classed as noble gas and a nonmetal.
It was discovered in 1895 by William Ramsay and Lord Rayleigh.

Number of Protons/Electrons: 18
Number of Neutrons: 22
Atomic Mass: 39.948 amu
Melting Point: -189.3 °C
Boiling Point: -186 °C
Classification: Noble Gas
Uses: Lighting
